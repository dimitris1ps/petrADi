Things to do

Beaches

Get your vitamin D from the sandy beach of Petra, or Anaxos, both walking distance.

Cultural Sites

Take the 114 steps to visit the historical church of Petra for a panoramic view of the area.

Castle of Molyvos

Just a 10 minutes drive to the adjacent village of Molyvos. Visit the castle at night for a memorable time.

Hiking

The area around Petra is full of valleys and maintains, ideal for the nature lovers.

Bird Watching

After a 20 minute drive you can connect with other bird watchers at Kalloni and enjoy the Flamingos.

Petra Village

Petra has atmospheric streets you can browse little shops and tour a small family owned Ouzo distillery.
